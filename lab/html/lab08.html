e1. differentiation and evaluation

syms x y
f = exp(x.^2+y.^2)
fx = diff(f,x)
fy = diff(f,y)
fxy = diff(fx,y)
fyx = diff(fy,x)

y = 3;

fprintf('The value of eval(fyx) is: %f\n', eval(fyx));
